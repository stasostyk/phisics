Why keep phisics up and running?

Even after two years since launching, phisics.tech maintains an active user base of students from around the world learning physics. With nearly 30,000 users since its launch, every day has more users coming in to learn something new.

In 2023, a consistent 400 students use the website every month with an average viewtime of around a minute and a half. Seeing 10 new connections from Nairobi appear as a teacher just told their students to open a phisics simulation inspires me to keep the site up and running.

Why should I support?

For complete transparency, I pay 40 euros per year to keep the website up, along with several hours of maintenance. Supporting me with just 2 euros will help me keep this up every year so that everyone has access to interactive simulations around the world. Thank you so much for your support!
